Embarking on the Journey of the Bald and Beautiful

◈Why I Decided to Cut My Hair◈

Back in the fall of 2019, I found myself with a haircut that seemed irreparable. Faced with desperation, I made a spontaneous trip to Great Clips, where I opted to have my hair shaved in the hopes of salvaging some semblance of style. To my surprise, I discovered a newfound sense of liberation and convenience with the absence of hair, prompting me to maintain the same style ever since. As the tumultuous year of 2020 unfolded with the onset of the pandemic, I took matters into my own hands and began to master the art of self-haircutting.

◈Pros and Cons of Shaving Your Head◈

  • Make never-ending the bald jokes!

CONS

  • People with long hair will make you miss your hair ☹
  • Limited versatility with styling your hair
  • Vulnerability to Cold Weather, the cold will hurt you head!
  • ...aside from these, the list of cons is virtually nonexistent!

◈Why I Will Never Grow Out My Hair Again◈

"When are you growing your hair back out?"

"Men like long hair!"

"Why would you do that?"

These questions echo in my daily interactions. Once, in the past five years, I let my hair grow, only to cut it all off again. The notion of having hair now feels peculiar. The laborious upkeep, the awkward transition phases, and the constant maintenance are all headache-inducing.

Being bald elevates my confidence to unprecedented heights. Embracing individuality, eschewing trends, and enjoying the simplicity of waking up without a hair routine are liberating. I've discovered myself with short hair and doubt I'll ever turn back.
